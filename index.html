Peak Heatwave Advisory Hits 12 Indian States — IMD Red Alerts Active
critical
What's happening
The India Meteorological Department has issued red and orange heatwave alerts across 12 states including Rajasthan, UP, Bihar, Odisha, and Telangana, with temperatures breaching 45°C in multiple districts as of the second week of May 2026. Heat-related hospital admissions are being reported in Nagpur, Lucknow, and Bhubaneswar, with state health departments issuing public dehydration and heat-stroke warnings.
