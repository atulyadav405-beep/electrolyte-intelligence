Science Credibility Is Becoming a Verifiable Consumer Expectation, Not a Marketing Claim
HIGH
What's happening
A structural shift is underway globally: premium electrolyte consumers — led by the US and UK markets but rapidly mirrored in India's Tier-1 urban cohort — are moving from trusting brand claims to demanding verifiable science.
