Peak Heatwave Alert: North India Temperatures Breach 46°C Threshold
critical
What's happening
IMD has issued red alerts across Delhi, Rajasthan, UP, and Haryana as May 2026 heatwave intensity rivals the historic 2024 peak, with multiple districts recording 45–47°C for consecutive days. Heat-related hospitalizations are spiking in urban centres, triggering state-level public health advisories urging citizens to hydrate aggressively throughout the day.
